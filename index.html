Overview

John Daghita, known online by the alias "Lick" or simply "John," is a 21-year-old American linked to one of the most high-profile insider cryptocurrency theft cases of the decade. He is accused of stealing over $40 million in cryptocurrency from U.S. government-controlled seizure wallets, with total on-chain connections to more than $90 million in suspected illicit flows. The case became public in January 2026 after blockchain investigator ZachXBT published a detailed thread connecting Daghita's online persona to wallet addresses holding stolen government funds.

The story is remarkable for its sheer recklessness. Daghita did not get caught through sophisticated law enforcement tracing or a whistleblower. He exposed himself by bragging about his crypto holdings in a Telegram group chat, screen-sharing his wallets for the world to see.
